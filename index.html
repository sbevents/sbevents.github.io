Frequently asked  questions

What types of events does Sadabahar specialize in?

Sadabahar specializes in a wide range of events, including weddings, corporate functions, social gatherings, fundraisers, and milestone celebrations. We have expertise in curating events for various occasions and can tailor our services to meet your specific needs.

How involved will I be in the event planning process?

At Sadabahar, we believe in collaboration and value your input throughout the planning process. We work closely with you to understand your vision, preferences, and requirements, ensuring that the event reflects your unique style and personal touch.

Can you assist with event design and decor?

Absolutely! Our team of skilled professionals includes experienced event designers who can conceptualize and create stunning decor to enhance the ambiance of your event. From floral arrangements to lighting, furniture, and thematic elements, we take care of every detail to transform your venue into a captivating space.

Do you offer customized catering services?

Yes, we can assist with customized catering services to suit your event theme and preferences. Whether you desire a specific cuisine, dietary accommodations, or unique presentation styles, our trusted catering partners work closely with us to create a delectable menu that delights your guests.

Can you handle event logistics and coordination?

Absolutely. We understand that flawless execution is essential for a successful event. Our team will take care of all the logistics, including vendor coordination, timeline management, and on-site supervision, ensuring a seamless and stress-free experience for you and your guests.

How far in advance should I book your services?

We recommend booking our services as early as possible to secure your preferred date and to allow ample time for planning and preparation. However, we also understand that some events may arise on shorter notice, and we will do our best to accommodate your needs based on availability.

What geographical areas do you serve?

While Sadabahar is based in Kathmandu, we provide event planning services throughout kathmandu valley and even outside kathmandu valley for special events. We are open to destination events as well and can discuss the logistics and details to ensure a successful event, regardless of the location.

In conclusion, Sadabahar is an exceptional event planning company that brings dreams to life with passion, attention to detail, and professionalism. From personalized experiences and exquisite decor to flawless execution and remarkable events, they exceed expectations and create cherished memories. Trust Sadabahar to transform your vision into a captivating reality.
